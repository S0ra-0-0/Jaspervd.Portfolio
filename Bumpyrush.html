Bumpyrush

Overview

Game Title: Bumpyrush

Genre: Party · Multiplayer · Casual

Team Size: 2-4 Players

Description — A chaotic circus-themed party game. Players race to collect 10 bear pickups before their rivals do, all while dodging each other and grabbing power-ups to gain an edge.

Key features

  • Free-for-all party gameplay for multiple players
  • First to collect 10 bears wins, keeping rounds fast and tense
  • Power-ups add chaos and comeback potential

Development

Gameloop

For this project my focus was on creating the main game loop and network syncing it across devices. I also made a leaderboard to see other player's progress.

It was my first expierience working with network programming using mirror so I had a lot to learn!

Power-ups

My second focus were implementing the power-up system to add variety and strategic depth to the gameplay. I designed a total of 8 uninque power-ups of which 5 made it into the final game.

Punch: Shoot a boxing glove at another player to knock them away!

Oil spill: Spill oil behind you, if a player runs into it, they will slide and fly away!

Size growth: Become larger and more intimidating, making it harder for others to knock you away!

Gambling: Flip a coin to either gain 3 bears or lose 2!

Vacuum: Use a vacuum to suck up bears and gain points!
